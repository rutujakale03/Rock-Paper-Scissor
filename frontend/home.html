What's inside

Everything you need to play

Built with Node.js, Express, MongoDB and vanilla JS — no frameworks needed.

🔐
Player Accounts
Register with a username and password. Your session is saved so you stay logged in across refreshes.
🏆
Live Leaderboard
See all players ranked by wins in real time. Your row is highlighted so you always know your position.
📜
Match History
Every round is saved to MongoDB. Your last 5 results are shown instantly after each game.
⚙️
Admin Dashboard
Admins can view all players, see full stats, win percentages, and delete accounts from a dedicated panel.
📊
Personal Score
Track your wins, losses and draws. Reset your own score anytime without affecting other players.
🎨
Modern UI
Animated backgrounds, glassmorphism cards, smooth transitions and full mobile responsiveness.
